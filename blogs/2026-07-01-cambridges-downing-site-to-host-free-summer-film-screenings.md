Anonymous (not verified) It is the first time the University has partnered with Screens in the City – delivered by Cambridge BID – to welcome Cambridge residents, workers, and visitors to this unique outdoor entertainment event. Over two days a giant screen on the Downing Site’s lawn will show eight different films, including Night at the Museum, School of Rock, and Matilda. Seating will be available on a first-come, first-served basis, and visitors are encouraged to bring along picnic blankets and deck chairs to make the most of the experience.
