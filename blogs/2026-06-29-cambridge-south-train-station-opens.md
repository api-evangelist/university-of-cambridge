Abby Munson The opening of Cambridge South train station marks a major milestone for the Cambridge Biomedical Campus and the University of Cambridge. It places the University teaching hospitals and research institutes on every route between Cambridge and London and provides direct connections to over 60 destinations across the UK. The new station will make it easier to attract and retain talent from across the UK and internationally.
